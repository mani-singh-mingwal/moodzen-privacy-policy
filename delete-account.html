Delete Your MoodZen Account

MoodZen is operated by Only for Solution. This page explains how to delete your account and associated data.

Delete your account in the app

You can delete your account directly from the MoodZen app:

  1. Open the MoodZen app and sign in.
  2. Open the side menu.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

After confirmation, we request deletion of your account and associated data from our servers at emotionhealer.com.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email onlyforsolution@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account. Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Subject line: Delete my MoodZen account
  • A request to permanently delete your account and associated data

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before processing the request.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete or de-identify, where applicable:

  • Account credentials (email and login data)
  • Profile information (name, address, phone, gender, age, profession, profile image URL)
  • Mood entries (mood type, intensity, notes, timestamps)
  • Emergency contacts saved in the app
  • Authentication tokens associated with your account

What may be kept temporarily

Some information may remain for a limited time in encrypted backups, security logs, or where required by law. Backup copies are typically removed within 90 days, unless a longer period is required by law or for dispute resolution.

Data stored on your device

Deleting your account does not automatically remove all local app data from your phone. You can uninstall the app or clear app data from your device settings if you want to remove locally stored information.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you want to use MoodZen again.
