1. Introduction

MoodZen (“the App”) is a mood-tracking and emotional wellness application operated by Only for Solution. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the App on Android or iOS, and when you use our services at emotionhealer.com (including our API).

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

Important: MoodZen is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Crisis helpline information in the App is for reference only and may open your device’s phone or messaging apps. In an emergency, call your local emergency number.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Data Purpose
Account credentials — email, password, full name Create and secure your account
Profile information — full name, address, phone, gender, age, profession, profile image URL Display and manage your profile
Mood entries — mood type, intensity, notes, timestamps Mood tracking, history, and charts
Emergency contacts — name and phone number Emergency & safety features
Password change details Account security
Password reset email Recover account access
Support messages Respond to support requests

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Purpose

Device and app data Troubleshooting and security
Push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging) Send notifications
Technical logs — IP address, timestamps, errors Fraud prevention and reliability

We use your device region/locale settings to show country-appropriate crisis helpline numbers. We do not collect precise GPS location.

2.4 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Precise GPS location

  • Payment or financial information
  • Apple Health, Google Fit, or wearable data

We do not sell your personal information or use mood data for third-party advertising.
